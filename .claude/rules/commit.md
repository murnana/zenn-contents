## Commit Convention

- Write commit messages in Japanese
- Initial article commit: ":sparkles: 新しい記事: 「<記事のタイトル>」を追加"
- Review fixes: ":pencil2: 記事のレビュー修正" with a detailed list of changes in the body
- Include English descriptions alongside Japanese when appropriate
